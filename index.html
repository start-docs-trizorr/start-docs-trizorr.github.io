Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I start my Trezor device?

To start your Trezor device, connect it to your computer and follow the setup guide at Trezor.io/start.

How do I secure my Trezor wallet?

Ensure your recovery seed is stored safely and use the passphrase feature for enhanced security.

What is a recovery seed?

A recovery seed is a list of words that can restore your wallet if it’s lost. Never share your seed with anyone.

Can I use Trezor with multiple wallets?

Yes, you can use Trezor with multiple wallets, all protected by your recovery seed and passphrase.

Where can I find more support?

For more support, visit Trezor Support.
